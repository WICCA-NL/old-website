Donate to WICCA

WICCA currently does not accept any cash donations.

If you wish to still donate something to make our events even greater, it can be in terms of:

  • Stickers (there’s never enough stickers!)
  • T-shirts and other WICCA goodies
  • Food and/or drinks for an event
  • Tickets to a large event for our members
  • … more creative things!

You can contact donate@womenofwicca.nl, we will be highly grateful!
