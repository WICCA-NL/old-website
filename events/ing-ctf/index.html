Wed, Nov 27, 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Location: ING HUB

HUB consists of 3 buildings: A, B and C. ING will organise this at the ground floor of the C-building, which is the long building facing Hotel Levell.

ING is inviting WICCA to their awesome open-for-all CTF!

How to teach anyone to be a hacker

Normally, Capture the Flag (CTF) events are organised for security enthusiasts only. This means that non-technical people are often overlooked and remain in the dark. In this session, ING will show you how you can use CTF events to teach anyone a basic level of hacking so they can defend themselves better against threats in their daily life.

During the evening, you will be put in the shoes of a hacker group. Together, you will solve the challenges that get you to the crown jewels of the bank. For this, you will use a mix of technical and analytical skills, but most important is to bring your hacker mindset.

Stay tuned, there will be a prize!
