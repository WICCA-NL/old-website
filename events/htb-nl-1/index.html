Hack The Box NL

Mon, Dec 09, 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Location: KPN Teleport

Hack The Box, the popular online platform to test and advance penetration testing skills, has partnered up with the WICCA team to organise meetups in the Netherlands, where everyone can go crazy inside a HTB lab and hack the planet!!!!!!1!

EVERYONE is welcome to join the HTB Meetup group, and especially WICCA members!

To participate to this hacking adventure, register to the HTB NL group above and sign up for our first event on December 9. This event is HTB-special.

Bring a laptop with your favourite hacking tools (Kali VM strongly advised). This evening, you will be introduced to the world of hacking, from initial comprise to full privilege escalation. You will have access to a private lab with two vulnerable machines. Sign up and get the hacking started!

The event will take place at the KPN office near Amsterdam Sloterdijk. This place is very accessible by car and public transport. Should you require a parking spot, let us know and we will try to get it arranged!

In order to enter the KPN building, you will need a valid identification document (ID, passport, driver’s license). We will need your first and last names to register you!
