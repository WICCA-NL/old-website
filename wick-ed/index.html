Wick-Ed

Wick-Ed is the WICCA initiative to teach aspiring female security newcomers about computer security. Wick-Ed courses include recorded introductory lessons on offensive security, binary exploitation, and more to come. The Wick-Ed collection of recorded lessons can be found on YouTube.
